# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)

## Do’s 

Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
Maintain a positive and professional online presence

## Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or post harmful comments
Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking sources
Don’t overshare details about your location or daily routine
Don’t post inappropriate content that could harm your reputation
